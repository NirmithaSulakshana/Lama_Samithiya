SHANTHI Cheildren's Society.

Annual Events

The Children's Society has been holding an annual jamboree since 1983. Its main objective is to provide an opportunity for children to develop their skills in the fields of dance, sports, singing, poetry, and drawing. Currently this is a Children's Day theme, with an invited volleyball tournament, drawing competitions, Poetry competitions are organized annually. There is an annual concert based on various themes. The program was conducted through the Zoom technology during the Covid era.

Annual Price Giving

Village Children who have been passing the Year 5 Scholarship Examination continuously since 1985 are invited and evaluated and given prizes.

Vesak Festival

Devotional song programs, Vesak pandal festivals and alms giving programs are conducted for the Vesak and Poson seasons.
