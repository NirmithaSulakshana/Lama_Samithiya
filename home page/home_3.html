SHANTHI Cheildren's Society.

Our Vision :-
Presenting a generation of virtuous children with morals.
Legal background :-
This is a sub society which is conducted under the supervision of the person who is appointed as the head of the children’s society in the annual general meeting of ‘Shanthi’ community center.
Registration :-
The society of Shanthi community council is registered annually as a community council under the section of society services and also it is registered annually as a sport society in AGA office, Attanagalla.

    • Annual operations.

    The children’s society has been holding an annual “Jamboriya” since 1986. The main intention of it is giving a chance to improve children’s talents in dancing,sport,singing,poetry,art work drawing fields.

    In present this is held themed on children’s day and also an invited volleyball tournament, drawing competition, singing the verse competition are organized annually. A concert is held annually based on various types of themes. This programme was held through zoom technology in covid season.

    The children who pass grade 5 scholarship have been appreciated and they have been given special gifts annually by the society since 1985.

    "Bakthi Geetha" programmes, Vesak pantheon festivals, alms giving programmes have been held for Vesak and Poson season.
    Financial strength :- As a society of children, collecting money is not done in this society .By the intervention of the leader of the children’s society, funds are supplied through the mother society “Shanthi” community council.
    Holding the general meeting :-
    The society should be gathered once a month. There is an authority to the leader and the officers of the society to gather members of meeting in a special occasion.
